🌾 RuralCare AI

AI-powered healthcare accessibility platform designed to bring intelligent medical assistance to rural and underserved communities.

Overview

RuralCare AI is a healthcare intelligence platform focused on improving access to preliminary medical guidance, symptom assessment, health education, and community healthcare support in rural regions.

The project addresses one of the largest healthcare challenges worldwide: limited access to medical professionals and healthcare resources in underserved areas.

The platform combines AI, symptom analysis, multilingual support, and community healthcare workflows to help users make informed healthcare decisions. The concept is similar to AI symptom-checking systems designed to improve healthcare accessibility.
